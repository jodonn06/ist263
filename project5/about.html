About Us

Who We Are

We are three college students who began In The Works during our first year of high school and have been working together for six years. Our diverse interests and experiences have allowed us to foster a creative work environment.

What We Do

We are located in the greater Philadelphia area and have reasonable rates for clients. By prioritizing the needs of our clients, we are able to produce professional-level videos for a wide array of filming needs. We have dedicated to serving our community. We produced a video for a local non-profit organization that was submitted to the Library of Congress. We have worked with our local school district to record performances for mass distribution. We value building and maintaining relationships with our clients to ensure that we produce the best possible product to suit their needs. We are interested in learning about new opportunities to improve our filming capabilities.
